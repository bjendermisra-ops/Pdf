Credit Utilization Tips

  • Keep utilization under 30% for optimal credit score.
  • Pay down balances to lower utilization.
  • Request credit limit increases (but don't spend more).
  • Utilization is calculated as (Total Balances / Total Limits) × 100%.

Credit utilization is a key factor in your credit score, typically accounting for 30% of FICO scores.
