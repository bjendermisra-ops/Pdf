SEO Writing Guidelines

Content Length

  • Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words
  • Product pages: 300-500 words
  • Landing pages: 500-1,000 words
  • Meta descriptions: 50-160 characters

Keyword Usage

  • Primary keyword in first 100 words
  • 1-2% keyword density (natural usage)
  • Use in H1, H2, and image alt text
  • Include semantic/LSI keywords

Readability

  • Aim for 8th-9th grade reading level
  • Paragraphs: 3-4 sentences max
  • Sentence length: 15-20 words average
  • Use bullet points and subheadings

SEO Best Practices

  • Use keywords naturally
  • Optimize for featured snippets
  • Include internal/external links
  • Mobile-friendly formatting
