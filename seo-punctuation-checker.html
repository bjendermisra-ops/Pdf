SEO Punctuation Guide

Periods (.)

  • End sentences with a single space after
  • No space before the period
  • Use in abbreviations (e.g., etc.)

Commas (,)

  • Use to separate items in a list
  • Place after introductory phrases
  • No space before, one space after

Quotation Marks (" ")

  • Straight quotes are preferred for web content
  • No space between quote and enclosed text
  • Punctuation goes inside quotes in US English

Hyphens (-) & Dashes (—)

  • Hyphens for compound words (e.g., well-known)
  • Em dashes for interruptions—like this—with no spaces
  • En dashes for ranges (e.g., 2010–2020)

Exclamation Points (!)

  • Use sparingly in professional content
  • Never use multiple (!!)
  • No space before, one space after

Ellipses (...)

  • Use exactly three dots with no spaces between
  • Space before and after when mid-sentence
  • No space before when at sentence end
