Understanding Net Worth

What are Assets?

Assets are what you own that has value. This includes cash, investments, property, and personal items that can be sold for cash.

What are Liabilities?

Liabilities are what you owe to others. This includes loans, credit card debt, mortgages, and other financial obligations.

Calculating Net Worth

Net Worth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities. This number represents your financial health at a specific point in time.

Why It Matters

Tracking your net worth over time helps you measure financial progress, make informed decisions, and work toward financial goals.
